Why PrivateAI

Three principles. Not marketing.

Here's what actually makes this different — and why it matters.

🎯

Quality that matches cloud AI — for your documents

When it comes to working with your own files — summarizing, searching, pulling out key info — local models hold their own against cloud AI. The only real difference is how fast you get the answer.

The tradeoff is speed, not quality. Local AI reads your documents just as well.

Every time you type into ChatGPT or Claude, that text goes to servers in the US. It can be logged. With PrivateAI, everything stays on your own computer — nothing leaves.
